1 · Overview

Where the agent sits, and what it owns

The Form Filler is the fourth of five agents — the assembler. Everything before it produces evidence (facts, diagnosis code, policy verdict); the Form Filler converts that evidence into the artifact an insurer actually receives: a completed, validated prior authorization form. It is the only agent that consumes all three upstream outputs at once.

Extractor ─────────────┐ ExtractedClinicalFacts│ ICD Coder ─────────────┤──► FORM FILLER ──► Critique (independent QA) ICDCodingResult │ │ Policy RAG ────────────┘ ▼ PolicyCheckResult PriorAuthForm (template id · fields[] · icd10_code · procedure_code · policy_id · validation_errors[])

Its design invariants:

Invariant 1 — Assemble, never invent Every field value is copied or mechanically derived from an upstream agent's typed output. The Form Filler contains no AI and no free-text generation: a value it cannot source is reported as a validation error — never fabricated.
Invariant 2 — Validation is the product The form carries its own defect list (validation_errors[]). A structurally complete form with unresolved conflicts still flows onward — but the orchestrator reads form.is_valid and routes the case to FAILED_VALIDATION instead of approval.

Why defects travel inside the form (not as exceptions) A hospital reviewer fixing a rejected case needs to see the whole form with the exact gaps marked — not a stack trace. Emitting a complete-but-annotated form preserves every piece of assembled evidence for the human who picks the case up.
6 · Security & auditability

Trust properties

  • Deterministic end-to-end: no LLM, no network, no randomness — identical inputs always produce byte-identical forms.
  • PHI: operates exclusively on the already-masked payloads; patient identifiers cannot reach a form field by construction.
  • Traceable values: every field's provenance is a typed upstream output; the procedure_code derivation is re-computable by auditors and is re-computed by the Critique Agent on every run.
  • Independent QA: Agent 5 re-validates form-vs-Extractor, form-vs-ICD, form-vs-Policy and the decision itself — a second pair of eyes that shares no code with the assembler.
  • Extensible by data: new insurer forms are added by appending a template (keywords + field list) to templates.json; new field types are one builder entry — no logic changes.
